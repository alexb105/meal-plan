7-Day Meal Plan

Day Checklist

Daily Schedule (Days 1-4 and 6-7)

Breakfast

4 Eggs (240g) scrambled with 1 cup (30g) of Frozen Kale sautéed in 1 Tbsp (15ml) Olive Oil

1 serving (200g) of Greek Yogurt

1 scoop (25g) of MyProtein Advanced Whey

Morning Snack

1 Can of Tuna (150g) (Days 1-4)

1 scoop (25g) of MyProtein Advanced Whey (Days 5-7)

1 Tbsp (16g) Peanut Butter

Lunch

150g Grilled Chicken Breast (Days 1, 3, 5, 7)

1 cup (90g) of Frozen Broccoli cooked with 1 Tbsp (15ml) Olive Oil

1 scoop (25g) of MyProtein Advanced Whey (Days 2, 4, 6)

Afternoon Snack

2 Hard-Boiled Eggs (120g)

1 serving (200g) of Greek Yogurt

1 scoop (25g) of MyProtein Advanced Whey

Dinner

150g Grilled Chicken Breast (Days 1, 3, 5, 7)

1 cup (90g) of Frozen Broccoli cooked with 1 Tbsp (15ml) Olive Oil

2 scoops (50g) of MyProtein Advanced Whey (Days 2, 4, 6)

Weekly Shopping List (Aldi)

  • Chicken Breast: 2kg (~£10.00)
  • Eggs: 3 dozen (~£3.75)
  • Canned Tuna: 4 cans (~£2.20)
  • Greek Yogurt: 1kg (~£2.80)
  • Peanut Butter: 300g (~£1.29)
  • Olive Oil: 250ml (~£1.25)
  • Frozen Kale: 500g (~£0.79)
  • Frozen Broccoli: 1kg (~£1.29)
  • MyProtein Advanced Whey: Enough for the week (~£6.00)

Meal Prep Tips

  • Batch Cook Chicken Breast: Grill or bake 2kg of chicken breast and divide into 150g portions.
  • Prep Vegetables: Cook the frozen kale and broccoli and store in the fridge.
  • Boil Eggs: Boil 3 dozen eggs in advance.
  • Protein Shakes: Have enough MyProtein Advanced Whey for the week.
